# Instagram/Facebook Pantry Essentials Template

Use for English Instagram/Facebook carousels built from source posts like:
`top X pantry essentials`, `grocery haul`, `restock`, `things to buy`, or
`what to get from an Asian supermarket`.

## Viral Mechanic

- Promise a useful saved list, not a hard ad.
- Show one product or category per page.
- Pair every item with a concrete use case.
- Keep the visual human: hand-held product, shelf/kitchen background, short
  sticker-like text blocks, and a casual creator POV.
- End by making the brand the convenient way to act on the list.

## Carousel Shape

Use the source page count when known. If the source says `top 7`, assume eight
pages unless visible evidence shows otherwise: one cover plus seven item pages.

1. Cover: `7 Asian pantry essentials worth keeping at home`
2. Everyday base: soy sauce, cooking sauce, stock, rice, or noodles
3. Flavor builder: chili flakes, chili oil, curry paste, or seasoning
4. Quick meal helper: dumplings, noodles, frozen buns, or instant soup base
5. Texture/snack: seaweed, rice cakes, crackers, mochi, or chips
6. Drink/dessert: tea, milk tea, jelly, soda, or dessert mix
7. Heavy/bulk staple: rice, oil, drinks, freezer items, or family packs
8. Brand convenience CTA: save the list, shop the set, or build a pantry box

## Umall Mapping

- Replace "local Asian supermarket" with `Umall` as the online way to get the
  same pantry logic at home.
- Avoid unsupported delivery claims, prices, or stock guarantees.
- Use "online Asian supermarket in Australia" unless the user explicitly asks
  to use a stronger brand claim from `brand-profile.md`.
- Favor red accents, clean shelf or kitchen scenes, visible Asian groceries,
  and a natural creator/hand-held product perspective.

## Copy Pattern

Keep each page under three short text blocks:

```text
[Item/category]
[Why it matters]
[How to use it]
```

Examples:

- `Soy sauce duo` / `Light for seasoning. Dark for colour.` / `One shelf, a lot
  of weeknight dinners.`
- `Chilli crunch or flakes` / `For heat, texture, and instant noodle upgrades.`
- `Frozen dumplings` / `Dinner backup for the nights you cannot be bothered.`
- `Rice, drinks, freezer staples` / `The heavy stuff is where online shopping
  starts making sense.`
