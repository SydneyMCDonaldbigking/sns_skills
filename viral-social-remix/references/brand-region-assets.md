# Brand Region Assets

Use this reference whenever a deliverable needs a visible company logo, table
sign, storefront sign, product packaging cue, or brand lockup.

## English Region

- Visible brand: `ASIAN GROCER ONLINE`
- Lockup: `ASIAN GROCER ONLINE`, with small `powered by UMALL`
- Logo file: `viral-social-remix/umall_logo/asian-grocer-online-powered-by-umall.png`
- Use for English videos, English Instagram/Facebook carousels, and
  Xiaohongshu source posts remixed into English.

## Chinese Region

- Visible brand: UMALL Chinese-region logo
- Logo file: `viral-social-remix/umall_logo/umall_logo.png`
- Use only for Chinese-region or Chinese Xiaohongshu deliverables.

## Placement Rules

- For English-region video storyboard frames 01 and 09, the physical brand prop
  should use the `ASIAN GROCER ONLINE` lockup. `powered by UMALL` may appear as
  the parent-company mark inside that same real-world prop.
- Do not use the Chinese-region UMALL logo for English-region deliverables.
- Do not turn either logo into subtitles, labels, lower-thirds, stickers,
  floating overlays, or ad banners.
- The logo can contain its real printed text as part of a physical object, but
  the video must still have no added captions, ingredient labels, title cards,
  or screen text.
